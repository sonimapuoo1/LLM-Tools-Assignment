iPhone

The iPhone is a line of smartphones designed and marketed by Apple Inc. It runs Apple's iOS mobile operating system.

MacBook

The MacBook is a line of Macintosh notebook computers designed and marketed by Apple Inc. It consists of a full-size keyboard, a machined aluminum case, and a thin light structure.

iPad

The iPad is a line of tablet computers designed, developed, and marketed by Apple Inc. It features a touch screen and is primarily used for internet browsing, media playback, and light computing tasks.
